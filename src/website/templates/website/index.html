About

Classroom is an interactive Student Assessment Tool for E-Learning and Classroom Response System. It is targeted towards a wide educational audience of Learners, Instructors, Developer and Expert Analyzer. It allows the learner access to learning materials and resources provided by the instructors. Also, it allows them to gain knowledge and closely monitor their learning path and activities. It allows the learners to share resources amongst each other for combined studies. It has a useful feedback system. Learners can also monitor and evaluate their progress.
